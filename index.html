Oden

Oden is a type of nabemono consisting of several ingredients such as boiled eggs, daikon or konjac, and processed fishcakes stewed in a light, soy-flavored dashi broth. Oden was originally what is now commonly called misodengaku or simply dengaku; konjac or tofu was boiled and eaten with miso

Main Ingredients

Main ingredients: Boiled eggs, daikon, konjac, fishcakes, soy-flavored dashi broth
